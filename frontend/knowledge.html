📚 Coffee Quality & Grading Knowledge

☕ What is Coffee Bean Grading?

Coffee bean grading is the process of evaluating beans based on texture, defects, size, color, and overall quality. Higher grades indicate premium beans suitable for export and specialty markets.

🌱 Types of Coffee

Coffea arabica: Premium quality, smoother flavor, higher market value.

Coffea canephora (Robusta): Stronger taste, higher caffeine content, commonly used in commercial blends.

🔬 Why Texture Matters?

In this system, texture features are extracted using advanced image processing techniques:

  • GLCM (Gray Level Co-occurrence Matrix) – Measures contrast, energy, homogeneity, and correlation.
  • LBP (Local Binary Pattern) – Captures micro-texture patterns of the bean surface.

These features help the machine learning model detect surface irregularities, defects, and quality variations automatically.
